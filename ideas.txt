Type:

- If condition (robot does once).
- While condition (robot does forever once condition met).
- Until condition (robot does form the start until condition met).

Condition:

- Button press
- Button drag to another button
- Mic (say word)

Robot Actions:

- Play audio from preset list (animal sounds, etc).
- Play song from preset list
- Play preset animation (animal eats, play, happy, sad, etc).
- Show image from gallery
- Play video/audio from gallery

Needed Activities:

From Conditions:
- Buttons (touchable, draggable) and a mic (for the SayWord condition).

From Actions:
- Buttons for Image, Video or Audio that open the media
  selection screens.

From Code.Play:
- Robot does what has been programmed to do.
